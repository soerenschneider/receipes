# Ingredients:

- 200g flour
- 60-75g sugar
- 175 butter
- 1 egg

## Optional:
- 2 tb matcha powder
- 1 vanilla pod

# Instructions:
1. Mix all the ingredients, knead thoroughly
1. Put it in the fridge for about 10m
1. Pre-heat the oven to 120Â°C, roll out the dough about ~5mm thick
1. Bake for 6-8 minutes
