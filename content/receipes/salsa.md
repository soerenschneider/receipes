# Ingredients
- 5 tomatoes, peeled
- 2 red bell pepper
- 2 green bell pepper
- 3 medium-large onion
- 2 chilli pepper
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 Tbsp salt
- 2 Tbsp sugar

# Instructions
1. Chop everything
1. Put the diced ingredients in a saucepan without a lid and cook over medium heat for about 1-1,5 hours, until the salsa has a creamy substance
1. Stir often
